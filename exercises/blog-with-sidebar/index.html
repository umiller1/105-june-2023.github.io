Naruto and Sasuke CAUGHT TOGETHER Having Dinner at Famous Romantic Restaurant in The Hidden Cloud on Boruto's Birthday!

by Ada Wong ★ Nov 3rd, 2023

In a shocking turn of events, our beloved Hokage Naruto Uzumaki and the enigmatic Sasuke Uchiha were caught having a candlelit dinner at the world-famous restaurant Soul Ties, and there's more to this than meets the eye. Naruto missed his own son Boruto Uzumaki's birthday on the very same day! The plot thickens, and the Konoha grapevine is buzzing with theories, rumors, and some very creative hashtags.

The Scene

On the evening of Boruto's eagerly anticipated birthday, Naruto and Sasuke were spotted by a vigilant citizen in the bustling heart of Konoha. They weren't on a mission to protect the village, nor were they seen doing any training. Instead, they were seated at a cozy, dimly lit corner table at Soul Ties, where lovebirds usually savor their most romantic moments.

Naruto's Alibi

Naruto Uzumaki, the Seventh Hokage, has some explaining to do. Missing your son's birthday is a serious offense, even for the Hokage! When confronted by our investigative ramen bowl, Naruto stammered, "I can't believe this. I thought today was tomorrow! Sasuke and I just wanted to discuss some ninja stuff, you know? It's all about teamwork, right?"

Naruto, despite his convincing story, couldn't hide the blush creeping up his whiskered cheeks as he wiped away some imaginary sweat from his forehead.

Sasuke's Defense

Sasuke Uchiha, ever the silent type, merely raised an eyebrow when questioned about this peculiar dinner date. He responded with an enigmatic, "Hn," which left everyone scratching their heads. But one thing is for sure; Sasuke doesn't seem concerned about the rumors circulating Konoha, and he's not talking.

The Gossip Around Town

The ninja grapevine is buzzing with speculation. Did Naruto and Sasuke go to Soul Ties to strengthen their "soul ties," or was it just a simple misunderstanding? While their wives, Hinata and Sakura, remain silent on the matter, some villagers insist that the two have finally given in to the undeniable chemistry that's been sparking between them since their earliest days at the Ninja Academy.

Naruto's birthday gaff aside, Boruto's feelings were genuinely hurt, and he was reportedly seen venting his frustrations with his pals Sarada and Mitsuki. "Dad never misses a chance to eat ramen, but he misses my birthday? Seriously?" Boruto exclaimed, shaking his head in disbelief.

Meanwhile, Sarada Uchiha, Sasuke's daughter, was observed writing a scathing letter to her father, questioning his loyalty to the village and his family, but the content of the letter remains classified.

Expert Opinions and the Future

We consulted some of Konoha's most prominent ninja to get their take on this scandal. Rock Lee offered some heartfelt advice, saying, "Naruto and Sasuke may have lost their way, but with the power of youth, they can find it again! I suggest they join me in some intense training to rekindle their true passions!"

Tenten, on the other hand, was more pragmatic. "Look, it could just be a misunderstanding, but they should definitely make it up to their kids. Maybe a Team 7 family picnic is in order!"
